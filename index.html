Pure Cleansing for Radiant Skin

Complete Skincare System

For best results, use both Glowtiqa products together as a complete skincare system. Start with our Premium Cleansing Soap to prepare your skin, then apply our Advance Care Whitening Cream for maximum effectiveness.

Our Whitening Cream is a dermatologist-tested formula designed to brighten the skin and give it a youthful, healthy glow. It works by targeting common skin concerns such as dark spots, uneven tone, scars, and fine lines.

With the power of TA stem cells and active skin-renewing ingredients, this cream supports deep nourishment while promoting skin regeneration. Regular use can visibly reduce dark circles, acne marks, wrinkles, and pigmentation.

Note: Avoid contact with eyes. In case of irritation, rinse thoroughly with water. If the irritation persists, consult a doctor immediately.

How To Use

  1. Start by cleansing your skin with Glowtiqa Cleansing Soap
  2. Pat skin dry with a clean towel
  3. Apply a small amount of the cream to the skin
  4. Massage it gently in circular motions until fully absorbed
  5. Use on face, neck, and other areas as needed
  6. For best results, apply at night before sleeping
