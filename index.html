Product Description

Glowtiqa Advance White Cream is a dermatologist-tested formula designed to brighten the skin and give it a youthful, healthy glow. It works by targeting common skin concerns such as dark spots, uneven tone, scars, and fine lines. With the power of TA stem cells and active skin-renewing ingredients, this cream supports deep nourishment while promoting skin regeneration.

Regular use can visibly reduce dark circles, acne marks, wrinkles, and pigmentation. It also softens the skin and enhances its natural brightness, making it appear fresher, smoother, and more radiant. Suitable for all skin types, Glowtiqa helps achieve noticeable results within a few days.

To use, start by washing the target area with clean water and a gentle face wash or soap. Apply a small amount of the cream and massage it into the skin in a circular motion. It can also be used on areas like the armpits, knees, and elbows. For best results, apply it at night.

Note: Avoid contact with eyes. In case of irritation, rinse thoroughly with water. If the irritation persists, consult a doctor immediately.
