Duo FAQs

Common questions about The Signature Glow Duo

How exactly should I use the duo daily?
Morning: Wash your face with the Skin Whitening Soap to remove overnight oils. Apply a light sunscreen (not included) before heading out.

Night: Wash your face again with the Skin Whitening Soap. Pat dry with a clean towel. Apply a small amount of the Advance Whitening Cream to your face and neck, massaging gently. Leave it on overnight.
Can I use the soap on my body as well?
Yes, the Skin Whitening Soap can be safely used on both the face and the body to clear dark spots, underarm darkness, or body acne.
Will the cream make my skin oily?
No, our French formulation is designed to be deeply hydrating yet lightweight and non-comedogenic, meaning it absorbs quickly without leaving a heavy, greasy residue.
What if I want faster, full-body results?
While the Duo is excellent for topical facial spots, if you are looking for faster, full-body whitening, we highly recommend upgrading to The Prestige Radiance Collection, which includes our 1200MG Booster Capsules to stop melanin production from the inside out.
